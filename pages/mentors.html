This page lists all the Mentors who have consented to be listed publicly on our web site. If you are a Mentor whose details do not appear here, but you would like to be listed, please update your Instructor profile in AMY and agree to make your profile public via the checkbox option within the record. To log in, select Login with GitHub and make and save your changes. If we do not yet have your GitHub username in your instructor record, please email it to us so we can add it and let you log in. If you add/have added your Twitter, GitHub, ORCiD, and personal website URL to your AMY record, they will appear as part of your record here. Find more information on AMY.
