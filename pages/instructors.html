Our Instructor Community

This page lists all the instructors who have consented to be listed publicly on our web site so far. If you are a badged Instructor whose details do not appear here, but you would like to be listed, please update your Instructor profile in AMY and agree to make your profile public via the checkbox option within the record. To log in, select Login with GitHub and make and save your changes. If we don't yet have your GitHub username in your instructor record, please email it to the team so they can add it and let you log in. If you add/have added your Twitter, GitHub, ORCiD, and personal website URL to your AMY record, they will appear as part of your record here. Find more information on AMY.
