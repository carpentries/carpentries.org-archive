Carpentries Maintainers work with the community to make sure that lessons stay up-to-date, accurate, functional and cohesive. Maintainers can expect to acquire this skill set. Find out more about becoming a Maintainer.

This page lists the Maintainers who have already consented to be listed on our website. If you are a badged Maintainer whose details do not appear here, but you would like to be listed, please update your Instructor profile in AMY and agree to make your profile public. Login with GitHub and save your changes. If you are unable to log in, please email the team.
